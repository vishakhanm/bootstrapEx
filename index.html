About Us

Nitin Mistry, founder of Om Interiors followed his father's footsteps and simply wanted good interiors. Instead, they got a rather appalling experience. It started with helping out in family business. It ended with the realization that even after spending a lot of time and money one could not be sure of a fair price, quality or even the final result.

Determined to never let any home or company owner experience the misery, he created what we now call Om Interiors. Launched in 1987 in India, Om Interiors is now the trusted brand for complete home interior design for thousands.
